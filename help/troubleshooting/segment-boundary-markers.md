---
title: Segment boundary markers visible/not-visible
---

To show or hide segment boundary markers, do the following:

1. On the **Graphs** menu, point to [View](../user-interface/menus/graphs/view), and then point to **Segment Boundaries**.
1. To *show* segment boundary markers, select **All Graphs**, **This Graph**, or **Waveform Only**.
1. To *hide* segment boundary markers, select **No Graphs**.
